About ECO-ROD LTD

ECO-ROD LTD is a trusted name in drain unblocking and cleaning services across Nottingham, Derby and Leicester. We specialise in providing efficient, professional solutions for domestic and commercial clients.

With years of experience, we use advanced technology including CCTV drain surveys, GPS mapping, and high-pressure drain jetting to diagnose and solve drainage problems quickly and effectively. Our team is fully trained to handle any drain-related issue, ensuring minimal disruption to your property or business.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. Whether you need emergency drain unblocking, routine maintenance, or a full-scale drainage investigation, we are here to help. Our services are available 24/7, so your drainage issues are resolved promptly, day or night.

We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and environmentally responsible drainage solutions, ensuring our methods comply with industry standards and regulations.
