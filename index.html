About Us

ECO-ROD LTD is a trusted name in drain unblocking and cleaning services. We specialize in providing efficient and professional solutions for domestic, commercial, and industrial clients.

With years of experience, we use advanced technology such as **CCTV drain surveys, GPS mapping, and high-pressure drain jetting** to diagnose and solve drainage problems quickly and effectively. Our team is highly trained to handle any drain-related issue, ensuring minimal disruption to your property or business.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. Whether you need emergency drain unblocking, routine maintenance, or full-scale drain investigation, we are here to help. Our services are available 24/7, ensuring that your drainage issues are resolved promptly.

At ECO-ROD LTD, we take pride in our commitment to sustainability and environmentally friendly drainage solutions. We ensure our methods comply with industry standards and regulations, providing you with reliable, long-term solutions.
