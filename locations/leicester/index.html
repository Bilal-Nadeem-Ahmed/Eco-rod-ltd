About Us

ECO-ROD LTD is a trusted name in drain unblocking and cleaning services in the Leicester. We specialize in providing efficient and professional solutions for domestic, commercial, and industrial clients.

With years of experience, we use advanced technology such as **CCTV drain surveys, GPS mapping, and high-pressure drain jetting** to diagnose and solve drainage problems quickly and effectively.
